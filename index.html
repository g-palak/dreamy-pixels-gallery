About Me

Hello! I'm Palak — a software engineer by profession and a creator by passion. By day, I write code and solve problems; by evening, I pick up a brush or a camera and do the same — just with different tools.

There's something deeply satisfying about building things, whether it's an elegant piece of software or a painting that finally captures the mood of a misty morning. Both demand patience, iteration, and a willingness to learn from mistakes.

My canvases tend toward cool hues — deep forest greens, dusky blues, the pale gold of late-afternoon light. Nature is my most recurring subject and my most reliable muse. Photography is how I freeze those fleeting moments of light and shadow.

Most of my paintings are inspired by references and artists I admire. Every brushstroke and every click is my own — a process of learning, experimenting, and slowly finding my voice.

⚠️ About the paintings

The paintings showcased in this gallery are personally created by me — every brushstroke is my own. However, most are inspired by or loosely referenced from existing artworks, photographs, and real-world scenes.

I do not claim originality of concept or composition for those works. This gallery reflects my learning journey as a self-taught painter — a space to practice, experiment, and grow by studying the work of artists I admire.

The photographs are original works taken by me. If you are an original creator and see your work referenced here and have concerns, please reach out via the Contact section.
